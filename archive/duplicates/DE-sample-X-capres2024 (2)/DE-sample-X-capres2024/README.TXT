Twitter/X Data Set for Researchers

In support of the advancement of statistics and data science, the Ministry of Education, Culture, Research, and Technology is organizing Statistika Ria and Festival Sains Data (SATRIA DATA). This event aims to foster networking opportunities between universities and the industry, in addition to promoting the scientific growth of statistics and data science.

As part of SATRIA DATA 2024, Drone Emprit has collaborated to provide a comprehensive Twitter/X dataset for research and competition purposes. This dataset focuses on conversations surrounding the 2024 presidential election, specifically concerning the three presidential candidates.

Dataset Specifications:
Total size: Estimated 3GB
Total records: Estimated 9,817,347
Period: January 2024
Keywords: "Anies" OR "Prabowo" OR "Ganjar"

This dataset is now available for researchers, lecturers, and students who wish to explore these conversations for academic or analytical purposes.
